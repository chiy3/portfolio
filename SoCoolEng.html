Challenges

English education in Asia focuses on reading and listening more than speaking and writing. High school students usually are capable of understanding English vocabulary and sentence but unable to express articulately or compose a sentence in English. After surveying 68 students first-grade high school students, we concluded the primary challenges of improving speaking and writing skills in English for students.

  • Time
    Students have to study 10+ subjects per day, so they have limited time for English.
  • Grade
    Students care about grade and English expression plays a minimal role in grade.
  • Fascination
    Content of textbooks are usually boring or impractical. Students barely read them only if necessary.

Competitive Analysis

To better understand current market and core values that users care, we analyzed six common methods for improving English expression skills.

User's Core Values

Deprived from survey, interview and comparative analysis, I determind three core value that user care the most.

  • Efficiency
    User desires a method to improve skills efficiently in tight schedule.

  • Positive Effect
    User expects a tool to bring them positive impact.

  • Down-to-earth
    User enjoys learning things that they can apply in dailty lfie.

Final Design

"How could we provide an efficient tool with down-to-earth content and positive effect to users?"

Based on interviews and surveys, we discovered that Youtube is popular media to get touch with English within users. However, most of users only regard it as an entertainment rather than a tool. Thus, we decided to start with making Youtube educational.

To reach our goal, we combined Youglish with textbooks. User can easily watch the film with vocabularies which included in textbooks. In this way, users not only learn the definition but also learn how to express them in a complete sentence.

Evaluation

To evaluate our project and gain feedback, we conducted a pretest-posttest with 2 classes. The total sample number was 68. According to the outcome, the correct rate of pronunciation increases the most (90.2%) and vocabulary comes in second place (80%). Moreover, the correct rate of grammar rises the least. We speculate that due to the complexity of grammar, it is difficult to have obvious enhance in grammar skills. Last but not least, the correct rate of expression climbs up from 34% to 58.6%. Although long-term evaluation is still needed, we agree that So Cool English has the potential to become even better in the future.
