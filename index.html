About Me

Who Am I?

Eric Chi, a current graduate student of Information Studies at The University of Texas at Austin. He has 3+ years experience in social design & research and 2+ years experience in front-end & web development.

With a background in social sciences and information science, my focus is all on users. This speaks to why I'm passionate about solving problems in both digital and real world for all users. My work combines user research, user-centered design and UI/UX development.

As a student and designer, I desire challenges and chances to push myself. I believe challenges are the best method of self-reflection, which is the key to outstanding design. My career goal is to deliver a simple, but wonderful experience to all users.

What can I do?

Design

Design is undividable from my daily life. With strong curiosity, I explore the designs in our world. As a UX designer, I relish interface design, graphic design and user experience design.

Research

Research is a method to see what everyone has seen and discover the things that no one has thought of before. I've conducted 100+ hours of field research including interviews and surveys

Coding

To me, coding is not only a powerful tool but also a new perspective on the world. From back-end to front-end, I've learned Python, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, PHP, Javascript and React.js.

Empathy

Empathy is all about standing in someone's shoes and seeing from other's perspectives. Regard myself as a user-centered designer, empathy is always my first priority.

Prototyping

Prototype is the intersection of idea and reality, and prototyping is the ability to give ideas life. With prototyping, I can exhibit my blueprints to others and test them over and over.
